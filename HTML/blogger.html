Lalisa Manoban

Lalisa Manobal (also spelled Manoban; born Pranpriya Manobal; March 27, 1997), known mononymously as Lisa is a Thai rapper, singer and dancer.
She is a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink, which debuted under YG Entertainment in 2016.
